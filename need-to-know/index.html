Need To Know

Twins in general, not just identical, have been a hot topic for centuries. Even back in the early days when science was just starting to be used, twins fascincated people. I can't personally blame them, especially identical twins. We do look pretty cool. Even today, my sister and I will be stared at on the subway, at school, or at work. Some people hide their shock and fascination a little better than others, but I am used to it by now. Many people don't know the difference between the many types of twins and some don't even know there are several types. If you would like to be educated on the world of twins, check out my other pages.
