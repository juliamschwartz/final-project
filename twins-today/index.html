Twins Today

There are so many events and activities for twins that many people have no idea about! They can be concerts, parades, festivals, and lectures just for twins! The largest and most well-known is a festival called Twin Days. As you can imagine, it is an event filled with set of twins, as well as triplets and other multiples. This festival is know all over the world and visited by sets of twins every year from different countries. Check it out!
