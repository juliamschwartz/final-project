Famous Literature

Scientists weren't the only ones who were interested in twins. Writers saw the unique appearence of twins and included characters in their novels, plays, and stories. Many famous authors such as William Shakespeare wrote The Twelfth Night back in the17th century. However, current authors such as J.K Rowling included a hilarious and mischevious set of twins in her immensely popular series Harry Potter.
